I’m a graduate researcher in AI/ML and generative AI (GenAI), with focus areas in explainable AI (XAI), computer vision, and applied analytics. I build models that are accurate, interpretable, and deployable—across domains like infrastructure, health, and education. Research Areas What I work on & why it matters

  • Explainability & Evaluation. Develop and audit model explanations (LIME, Grad‑CAM) for CNNs/Transformers; quantify faithfulness via deletion/insertion AUC, monotonicity, and comprehensiveness; probe failure modes and calibration.
  • Vision for Infrastructure. Build end‑to‑end UAS/UAV pipelines for concrete crack detection: dataset protocols, class imbalance handling, illumination/texture normalization, and real‑world constraints (motion blur, scale, background noise).
  • Efficient Vision Transformers. Design and evaluate Residual Vision Transformers with ablations on tokenization (patch size/stride), depthwise residual projection, positional encoding, and training/regularization schedules.
  • Analytics & Data Systems. Create reproducible ETL/ELT workflows in SQL/Python, apply data & model versioning, and ship lean BI dashboards that drive decisions.
