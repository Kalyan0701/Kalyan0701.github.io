About

Researcher, builder, and lifelong learner.

I'm Kalyan Khatry and I am a researcher passionate about making AI systems that are not only powerful, but transparent, fair, and robust enough to trust in the real world. My work sits at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, and civil infrastructure.

As a Graduate Research Assistant at the University of New Haven, I developed custom vision transformer architectures for automated bridge deck inspection using UAV-collected imagery, and integrated explainable AI methods to make these models interpretable for engineers and decision-makers.

Beyond research, I value peace, nature, and positive energy. I believe that good science emerges from a grounded mind and a clear purpose.
